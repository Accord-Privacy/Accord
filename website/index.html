How It Works

A simple architecture with zero trust assumptions.

1

Relays Are Dumb Pipes

Relays route encrypted blobs between clients. They can't read, log, or analyze your content. If a relay is compromised, your messages stay safe.

2

Nodes Are Communities

Each community runs as a Node with its own channels, roles, and settings. Nodes connect through relays but own their own data.

3

Everything Is E2EE

Messages are encrypted on your device and decrypted on the recipient's. Keys never leave your machine. It's that simple.
